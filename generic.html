As a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering, I am passionate about leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and medical technology to drive innovation in healthcare. My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, medical technology, and brain-computer interfaces (BCI), with a focus on advancing healthcare solutions.

During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to conduct a significant research project focused on the application of machine learning in breast cancer classification, which culminated in my bachelor's thesis. This project not only honed my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of the critical role that technology plays in medical diagnostics. I am driven by a strong desire to contribute to the development of cutting-edge solutions that have a meaningful impact on healthcare outcomes. I am eager to collaborate with interdisciplinary teams and industry professionals to further explore the potential of AI and medical technology in addressing complex healthcare challenges. Let's connect and explore opportunities to drive positive change at the intersection of engineering, AI, and healthcare.
